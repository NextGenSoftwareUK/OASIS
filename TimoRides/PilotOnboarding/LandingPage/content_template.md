## What is the TimoRides × PathPulse partnership?

- A collaboration between TimoRides and PathPulse to collect real-time route and traffic data while drivers stay on the road.
- PathPulse adds intelligent routing and data visualization on top of Timo’s driver network.
- The pilot supports future South African mobility intelligence goals while building on PathPulse’s work with the Indian government.
- Data helps shape better routing, more accurate ETAs, and a stronger driver experience.

## Exclusive leaderboard competition

- $1000 prize pool across multiple stages: top 20, top 15, and top 10 earn cash rewards throughout the 15-day competition (December 5–20).
- Drivers are ranked by app activity and the quality of the data they capture; winners are announced after each phase.
- Stay active on the PathPulse app, monitor the live leaderboard, and take early action—drivers can start building momentum before the official launch day.
