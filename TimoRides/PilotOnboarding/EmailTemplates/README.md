# Email Templates for PathPulse Pilot

## Purpose
Professional email communications for driver onboarding and ongoing support.

## Files to Create

### `welcome_email.html`
Initial welcome email with program overview

### `onboarding_instructions_email.html`
Step-by-step onboarding guide

### `reminder_email.html`
Follow-up reminders for incomplete onboarding

### `incentive_update_email.html`
Updates on rewards and performance

### `support_email.html`
Support and troubleshooting information

### `launch_day_email.html`
Special email for December 1st launch

---

## Email Schedule

- **Day -7:** Welcome email
- **Day -5:** Onboarding instructions
- **Day -3:** Reminder email
- **Day -1:** Final instructions
- **Day 0 (Dec 1):** Launch day email
- **Ongoing:** Weekly updates and reminders
