Connect to Holochain
with HoloNET
HoloNET allows any .NET application to port to Holochain, offering scalable, affordable, secure and decentralised cloud computing.

Visit HoloNET
Thanks to our bridge software HoloNET, we allow any .NET application to port to Holochain, offering scalable, affordable, secure and decentralised cloud computing.


HoloNET is designed to integrate seamlessly with The OASIS, giving you the benefits of this revolutionary technology without the headache of understanding how it works.

For state-intensive projects this is highly significant. By connecting the wider web3 world through to Holochain, we open the possibility of fully on-chain gaming, or fully decentralised enterprise-scale infrastructure - removing the need for AWS.

Here to support the projects of the future
Thanks to a highly robust architecture where the sum is greater than the parts. Apps using the OASIS can enjoy benefits such as offline functionality, zero downtime, and best-in-class performance, stability and security.
Auto failover, Auto load-balance, Auto replication
OASIS automatically finds the fastest node to connect to and can switch providers based on your preference. Meaning if your main provider goes down, switch to the next with no drop in performance.
Zero Downtime
Because OASIS jumps to the next best provider or node, it keeps service up even when a whole network goes down. Giving your app unprecedented reliability.
Advanced Error Handling
OASIS diagnostics make bug fixing as streamlined and intuitive as possible, allowing you to troubleshoot faster.
Offline Functionality
OASIS apps work locally without internet connection- and resync when online again.
Automatic Upgrades
OASIS handles upgrades for all providers for you. Saving yet more time.
Future-proof by default
Whenever OASIS adds a new protocol or provider, you get access.
