## Presentation Tips

Before the Pitch:
1. Practice 5+ times
2. Time to exactly 10 minutes
3. Prepare for technical questions
4. Test demo video
5. Know your audience

During the Pitch:
1. Start with energy
2. Show, don't tell (use demo)
3. Make it personal
4. Be humble but confident
5. Invite collaboration
6. End memorably ("The answer is 42")

Handling Q&A:
- Technical questions: Have backup slides ready
- "Why H2G2?": Cultural bridge + untapped audience
- "Why you?": 4+ years production experience
- "Why $42K?": Perfect meme + budget breakdown
- "What if you fail?": Open-source everything
- "Competitors?": Tech exists, culture doesn't
