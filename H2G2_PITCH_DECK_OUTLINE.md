## ğŸ¤ Presentation Tips

### **Before the Pitch:**
1. **Practice** the full presentation 5+ times
2. **Time it** to exactly 10 minutes (leave 5 for Q&A)
3. **Prepare** for technical questions (have appendix ready)
4. **Test** demo video and all media
5. **Know your audience** (research the foundation)

### **During the Pitch:**
1. **Start with energy** ("We're building the actual Hitchhiker's Guide!")
2. **Show, don't tell** (use demo video, live if possible)
3. **Make it personal** ("I grew up reading H2G2...")
4. **Be humble** but confident (acknowledge competitors)
5. **Invite collaboration** ("We want YOUR chain featured in The Guide")
6. **End memorably** ("The answer is 42. Will you help us build it?")

### **Handling Q&A:**
- **Technical questions:** Have appendix slides ready
- **"Why H2G2?":** Cultural bridge + untapped audience

- **"Why $42K?":** It's the perfect meme + budget breakdown
- **"What if you fail?":** Open-source everything, community owns it
- **"Competitors?":** Tech exists, culture doesn't

- âŒ Don't read slides word-for-word
- âŒ Don't skip the demo
