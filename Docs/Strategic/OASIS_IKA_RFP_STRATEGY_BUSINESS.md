## Understanding the Ika RFPs

### What Are These RFPs About?

Ika is looking for teams to build 12 different financial and blockchain applications. Each one uses their new "dWallet" technology, which is like a super-secure digital safe that:

- Works across multiple blockchains (Bitcoin, Ethereum, Sui, etc.)
- Requires two keys to open (user key + network key) for maximum security
- Can be controlled by smart contracts (programmable rules)
- Never gives full control to any single party (zero-trust)

Think of it like a bank vault where:
- The customer has one key
- The bank has another key
- Both keys are needed to open the vault
- Smart contracts (automated rules) control when the vault can be opened
