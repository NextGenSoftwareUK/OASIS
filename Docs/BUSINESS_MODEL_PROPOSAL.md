# OASIS Business Model Proposal

**Date:** January 18, 2025  
**Purpose:** Strategic Business Model Definition  
**Status:** Proposal for Advisory Review

---

## Executive Summary

OASIS should adopt a **multi-tier, multi-revenue-stream business model** that combines:
1. **SaaS Subscriptions** (Foundation) - Predictable recurring revenue
2. **Usage-Based Pricing** (Scale) - Pay-per-use for high-volume customers
3. **Enterprise Contracts** (Premium) - Custom deployments and government deals
4. **Transaction Fees** (Network Effects) - Revenue from platform transactions
5. **Marketplace Revenue** (Ecosystem) - Agent marketplace, NFT marketplace, data marketplace

**Target:** $10M ARR in Year 1, $50M ARR in Year 2, $200M+ ARR in Year 3

---

## Core Business Model: Hybrid SaaS + Usage-Based

### Tier 1: Developer/Startup Tier (Freemium Foundation)

| Tier | Price | API Calls | Storage | Features | Target Market |
|------|-------|-----------|---------|----------|---------------|
| **Free** | $0 | 1,000/month | 100 MB | Read-only, community support | Developers, students, hobbyists |
| **Bronze** | $9/month | 10,000/month | 1 GB | All read operations, basic writes | Small projects, startups |
| **Silver** | $29/month | 100,000/month | 10 GB | All operations, priority processing | Growing businesses |
| **Gold** | $99/month | 1,000,000/month | 100 GB | Advanced features, analytics | Established companies |

**Revenue Projection:**
- Year 1: 10K free, 1K Bronze, 500 Silver, 100 Gold = **$74,900/month = $898,800/year**

## Revenue Streams

### 1. SaaS Subscriptions (Primary - 60% of revenue)

**Structure:**
- Monthly/annual subscriptions
- Tiered pricing based on usage
- Auto-renewal with credit card
- Annual discounts (15-20% off)

**Key Features:**
- Predictable recurring revenue
- Low churn (high switching costs)
- Scalable (marginal cost per customer is low)
- Easy to forecast

**Target:** $10M ARR Year 1, $50M ARR Year 2, $200M ARR Year 3

---

### 2. Enterprise & Government Contracts (High-Value - 30% of revenue)

**Structure:**
- Custom pricing based on requirements
- Multi-year contracts (2-5 years)
- Implementation fees ($50K-$500K)
- Ongoing support and maintenance

**Key Features:**
- High contract values ($50K-$500K/month)
- Long-term commitments
- Strategic partnerships
- Government adoption for holonic architecture

**Target:** $7.8M Year 1, $36M Year 2, $156M Year 3

---

### 3. Transaction Fees (Network Effects - 5% of revenue)

**Structure:**
- Small percentage on platform transactions
- Cross-chain transfers: 0.1% fee
- Agent-to-agent payments: 1-2% platform fee
- NFT marketplace: 2.5% transaction fee

**Key Features:**
- Scales with platform usage
- Network effects (more users = more transactions)
- Low friction (built into platform)
- High margin

**Target:** $600K Year 1, $6M Year 2, $24M Year 3

---

### 4. Marketplace Revenue (Ecosystem - 3% of revenue)

**Structure:**
- Agent marketplace: 10-15% commission on agent services
- NFT marketplace: 2.5% transaction fee
- Data marketplace: 20-30% revenue share
- Template/component marketplace: 30% revenue share

**Key Features:**
- Ecosystem growth
- Platform lock-in
- Network effects
- Community-driven

**Target:** $300K Year 1, $3M Year 2, $12M Year 3

---

### 5. Professional Services (Services - 2% of revenue)

**Structure:**
- Implementation services: $150-$300/hour
- Custom development: Project-based
- Training and consulting: $2,000-$10,000/day
- Support contracts: $5K-$50K/year

**Key Features:**
- High margin
- Strategic relationships
- Upsell opportunity
- Knowledge transfer

**Target:** $200K Year 1, $2M Year 2, $8M Year 3

## Pricing Strategy

### Freemium Model (Customer Acquisition)

**Free Tier Benefits:**
- Low barrier to entry
- Viral growth potential
- Builds user base
- Upsell opportunity

**Free Tier Limits:**
- 1,000 API calls/month
- 100 MB storage
- Read-only operations
- Community support
- Watermarked outputs (optional)

**Conversion Strategy:**
- In-app upgrade prompts
- Usage-based triggers ("You've used 80% of your free tier")
- Feature gating (premium features require upgrade)
- Email campaigns

---

### Value-Based Pricing (Enterprise)

**Pricing Factors:**
1. **Data Volume:** Storage and processing requirements
2. **API Usage:** Request volume and complexity
3. **Support Level:** Response time, SLA requirements
4. **Custom Features:** White-label, on-premise, custom integrations
5. **Compliance:** Government, healthcare, financial regulations

**Enterprise Pricing Examples:**
- **Small Enterprise:** $5,000/month (100M API calls, 1TB storage)
- **Mid Enterprise:** $20,000/month (1B API calls, 10TB storage, SLA)
- **Large Enterprise:** $50,000/month (Unlimited, on-premise option)
- **Government:** $200,000-$500,000/month (Mass-scale storage, compliance)

---

### Government-Specific Pricing (Holonic Architecture)

**Value Proposition:**
- 50-70% storage reduction
- 60-80% compute reduction
- $10B+ potential global savings
- Complete audit trails
- Data sovereignty

**Pricing Model:**
- **Per-Petabyte:** $1,000-$5,000/month per petabyte stored
- **Per-Citizen:** $0.10-$0.50/month per citizen record
- **Fixed Contract:** $200K-$500K/month for national deployments
- **Implementation Fee:** $500K-$2M one-time

**Revenue Projection:**
- Year 1: 2 countries × $200K/month = **$4.8M/year**
- Year 2: 5 countries × $300K/month = **$18M/year**
- Year 3: 10 countries × $500K/month = **$60M/year**
