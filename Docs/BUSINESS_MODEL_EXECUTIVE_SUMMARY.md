# OASIS Business Model: Executive Summary

**Date:** January 18, 2025  
**For:** Advisory Group Review

---

## Recommended Business Model

**Hybrid SaaS + Usage-Based + Enterprise + Government**

A multi-revenue-stream model combining:
1. **SaaS Subscriptions** (60% of revenue) - Predictable recurring revenue
2. **Enterprise Contracts** (30% of revenue) - High-value customers
3. **Transaction Fees** (5% of revenue) - Network effects
4. **Marketplace Revenue** (3% of revenue) - Ecosystem growth
5. **Professional Services** (2% of revenue) - Strategic relationships

---

|------|-------|----------|

## Key Revenue Streams

### 1. SaaS Subscriptions (Primary)
- **Structure:** Monthly/annual subscriptions
- **Target:** $898K Year 1 → $3.6M Year 2 → $14.4M Year 3
- **Advantages:** Predictable, scalable, low churn

### 2. Enterprise Contracts (High-Value)
- **Structure:** Custom pricing, multi-year contracts
- **Target:** $7.8M Year 1 → $36M Year 2 → $156M Year 3
- **Advantages:** High contract values, long-term commitments

### 3. Transaction Fees (Network Effects)
- **Structure:** Small % on platform transactions
- **Target:** $600K Year 1 → $6M Year 2 → $24M Year 3
- **Advantages:** Scales with usage, high margin

### 4. Marketplace Revenue (Ecosystem)
- **Structure:** Commissions on agent services, NFTs, data
- **Target:** $300K Year 1 → $3M Year 2 → $12M Year 3
- **Advantages:** Network effects, platform lock-in

### 5. Professional Services (Strategic)
- **Structure:** Implementation, consulting, training
- **Target:** $200K Year 1 → $2M Year 2 → $8M Year 3
- **Advantages:** High margin, strategic relationships
