## Glif-Specific Technical Style Guidelines

### Visual Elements:
- **Holon Icons**: Geometric shapes (cubes, hexagons) with subtle 3D effect
- **Connection Lines**: Glowing, animated where appropriate
- **Provider Icons**: Recognizable logos where possible, or stylized icons
- **Data Streams**: Particle effects, flowing animations
- **Architecture Diagrams**: Clean, layered, professional

### Color Scheme:
- **Primary**: Blue (#4A90E2) - Trust, technology
- **Secondary**: Purple (#7B68EE) - Innovation, creativity
- **Accent**: Green (#2ECC71) - Success, performance metrics
- **Background**: Dark blue/gray (#1A1F2E) or white with subtle gradients

### Typography:
- **Headers**: Bold, sans-serif (Inter Bold, 24-32pt)
- **Body**: Regular, sans-serif (Inter Regular, 14-16pt)
- **Code**: Monospace (Fira Code, 12-14pt)
- **Labels**: Small, uppercase (Inter Medium, 10-12pt)

### Technical Diagram Conventions:
- **Holons**: Hexagonal or cubic shapes
- **Flows**: Left-to-right or top-to-bottom
- **Layers**: Stacked, clearly separated
- **Connections**: Solid lines for direct, dashed for indirect
- **Data**: Animated particles or streams
