# Ascension Pool — Site Brief (for web/agent)

**Purpose:** Add “Ascension Pool” to the site in a way that’s clear and on-brand. Use this brief for copy, sections, and any UI that references the pool.

---

## What it is (one line)

**Ascension Pool** is the shared treasury that sends **blessings** (SOL) to Saint NFT holders who also hold $SAINT — on a schedule (e.g. weekly or monthly).

---

## Naming and tone

- **Pool name:** **Ascension Pool** (preferred). Alternatives in docs: Saint Treasury, Blessings Pool.
- **Payments to users:** Call them **blessings** (e.g. “Receive blessings from the Ascension Pool”, “Weekly blessings”).
- **Who gets paid:** “Saint NFT holders who hold $SAINT” — both are required. Don’t say “only NFT holders” or “only $SAINT holders.”

---

## Value proposition (for site copy)

- **Hold a Saint NFT** = you’re in the set that *can* earn.
- **Also hold $SAINT** = you qualify to receive the payout. No $SAINT in wallet → no payout.
- **Ascension Pool** is funded by fees, donations, revenue share, and treasury; it distributes to that qualifying set on a schedule.

Keep it simple: “Hold your Saint NFT and $SAINT to receive blessings from the Ascension Pool.”

---

## What the site can show

1. **Section / feature name:** e.g. “Ascension Pool” or “Blessings from the Ascension Pool”.
2. **Short description:** 1–2 sentences using the one-line definition and value prop above.
3. **Donate / fund the pool:** If we publish the pool’s Solana address, the site can show it with copy like “Fund the Ascension Pool” or “Send SOL to the pool” (plus the address and maybe a QR).
4. **Eligibility reminder:** “To receive blessings: hold at least one Saint NFT and $SAINT in your wallet.”

---

## What not to imply

- Don’t say only NFT holders get paid (they must also hold $SAINT).
- Don’t say only $SAINT holders get paid (they must also hold the Saint NFT).
- Don’t promise specific amounts or dates unless product has committed to them.

---

## Summary for the agent

- **Ascension Pool** = treasury that pays “blessings” (SOL) to **Saint NFT holders who also hold $SAINT**.
- Use the word **blessings** for those payments.
- Site can have a section for Ascension Pool, a short description, optional donate/fund CTA with pool address, and an eligibility line (Saint NFT + $SAINT).
