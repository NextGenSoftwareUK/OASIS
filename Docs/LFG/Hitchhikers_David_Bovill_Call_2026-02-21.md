## 3. IP pool / commons model (David B’s proposal)

### 3.1 Principle

- **Hitchhiker’s IP** and **OASIS IP** (and e.g. Lexon) treated similarly: value the IP commercially (e.g. “if we sold to Elon Musk”), put it into **public benefit / commons**, and use **tithing** to pay back contributors over time. When paid off, IP is fully in the commons (with possible ongoing tithing for infrastructure).
- **OASIS:** Agreement with David (e.g. 70/30 or similar), value the IP, David (and Max) get **capped** return via tithing, not infinite. David remains CEO/CTO of Next Gen; gets salary/fellowship; funded by board for deep research; “king/principle” of the kingdom. Next Gen World Limited **retains what it is** but **no longer holds the IP** — IP is in the pool under contract.
- **Governments:** Can be told “this is a not-for-profit commons; IP holders are being paid back over time; you can trust this for critical infrastructure.” Clear **licensing package** (code, brand, etc.). LFG can sell rights to governments from a clean IP structure.
- **Spinoffs:** Three types: (1) protect my venture’s IP and it goes into commons, (2) fully private but license OASIS/Hitchhiker’s bits, (3) hybrid (e.g. Uniswap-style). Commercial licensing layer for products using OASIS / Hitchhiker’s IP.
- **Automation:** Optional use of blockchain (e.g. Uniswap v4–style hooks) for licensing / tithing; can keep it simple initially.
- **Cost:** Full legal setup often costs millions. Strategy: stages, trusted boards, pro bono / good legal contacts, move as fast as possible without big budget.

### 3.2 Structure (high level)

- **IP holding vehicle** (e.g. Third System or similar) — contractual; IP in; tithing out.
- **Next Gen World Limited** — unchanged in day-to-day; David still CEO/CTO; now **licenses** from the pool instead of owning IP.
- **Steering board** — one board to start; may later create separate foundations (Hitchhiker’s, Lexon, OASIS).
- **Research foundation / fellowship** for David — funded to do protocol research.
- From the moment IP is in the pool, **many companies** can license and do JVs; Max’s two existing projects become ecosystem members licensing OASIS; Max has business freedom and repayment guarantee.

### 3.3 What to document

- David B to write up: IP pool in more detail, how it works, who to involve, approach for support. Share with Max so Max can due-diligence.
- Then: bring in legal people (IP law firm + contacts), then align LFG.
