# How What We're Building Matches Story Protocol

**Reference:** [Story Protocol](https://www.story.foundation/) — “AI-native infrastructure for IP”: tokenize IP, make it programmable (ownership → remix → monetization), with programmable licensing and onchain enforcement.  
**Purpose:** Map OASIS + IP commons to Story’s model so we can articulate fit and possible integration.

---

## 1. What Story Protocol Does (summary)

From [story.foundation](https://www.story.foundation/):

- **Positioning:** AI’s next leap depends on unlocking the ~$80T IP asset class. Story tokenizes IP and makes it **programmable** (ownership, remix, monetization).
- **Programmable licensing:** Machine-readable, legally enforceable licenses, ready for autonomous workflows. Tiers: Open Use → Non-Commercial Remix (credit) → Commercial Use (paid) → Commercial Remix (paid + credit).
- **IP tokenization:** Tokenize, stake, trade, and combine IP into tradable portfolios.
- **Onchain enforcement:** Automated **attribution and royalties** across **derivative graphs** (parent → remixes).
- **Use cases:** AI companies (rights-cleared data), data providers, biomedicine/patents, institutional funds, brands/entertainment, creators.

Story is the **infrastructure layer** that makes IP and real-world data programmable, enforceable, and monetizable.

## 3. Differences (and why they’re useful)

| Dimension | Story | OASIS + IP commons |
|-----------|--------|---------------------|
| **Primary layer** | Onchain protocol: tokenize IP, programmable licenses, derivative graphs, enforcement onchain. | **Data + governance:** Holonic architecture, global graph, **commons** as IP holder, **tithing** as payback; **optional** onchain for tithing/enforcement. |
| **Primary users** | Creators, brands, AI companies, data providers, funds (tokenized IP, remix, royalties). | **B2G**, sovereign data, **governments**, OpenSERV/Hitchhiker’s/LFG ecosystem, **commercial licensees**; creators/contributors via tithing. |
| **Trust anchor** | Protocol + onchain state. | **Not-for-profit commons** + board + contracts; onchain as enhancement. |
| **Geography / sector** | Broad: AI, DePIN, biomedicine, entertainment, creators. | Strong in **government, institutional, creative economy** (Hitchhiker’s, LFG) and **AI infrastructure** (BRAID, OpenSERV). |

So: **Story = protocol layer for programmable IP and derivatives.** **OASIS = data/interop + stewardable IP commons + licensing.** They can **complement** each other: Story could be the **onchain licensing/royalty layer** for OASIS IP (or for derivatives of it); OASIS could be the **data and governance layer** that holds and licenses IP that is then tokenized or enforced via Story.

---

## 4. One-line summary

**What we’re building matches Story in goal (programmable IP, creator payback, derivatives, AI-ready data) and differs in emphasis: we center a not-for-profit IP commons and tithing for B2G and institutions, with optional onchain automation — and that same IP could be exposed or enforced through a protocol like Story.**

---

## 5. Possible integration (for later)

- **Commons ↔ Story:** IP held in the OASIS/Hitchhiker’s commons could be **registered or mirrored** on Story for programmable licensing and derivative graphs (e.g. commercial remix terms, attribution, royalties).
- **Tithing ↔ Story:** Our **tithing** (payback to creators) could be **recorded or executed** via Story’s royalty/attribution machinery so it’s machine-readable and onchain.
- **Narrative:** “OASIS + Hitchhiker’s commons holds and stewards the IP; Story makes it programmable and enforceable onchain for remix and royalties” — clear split of roles for partners and funders.
