### Data Duplication Elimination

#### Duplication Problem
- **Conventional systems**: 3-5x duplication factor
- **ROT data**: 30-60% of total storage
- **Copy data**: 60% of storage (backups, snapshots)
- **Result**: For every 1 unit of unique data, conventional stores **10 units** total

#### Holonic Solution
- **Unique data**: Stored once (no duplication)
- **Overhead**: 0.1% per relationship
- **Intelligent backups**: 10% (vs. 300% in conventional)
- **Result**: For every 1 unit of unique data, holonic stores **1.1 units** total

#### Efficiency Ratio
- **Conventional**: X × 10 (10x duplication)
- **Holonic**: X × 1.1 (minimal overhead)
- **Efficiency**: **9x more storage-efficient**
