# FAQ: OASIS Major Milestones (2011 to Present)

**Question:** OASIS dates back to 2011. What are the major milestones from then to now?

**Answer:**

OASIS has evolved from an early vision in 2011 into a comprehensive Web4/Web5 infrastructure. Here are the major milestones:

## **2011 - The Beginning**
- Initial R&D, research, design, and prototyping begins for "Our World" (the digital twin of our planet)
- Foundation laid for what would become the universal OASIS platform

## **2017 - Public Launch**
- Public introduction of the Our World/OASIS vision
- Initial vision and architecture design phase begins
- Community building starts

## **2018 - Partnerships & Prototypes**
- Partnership with Be Earth UN-IGO (United Nations Intergovernmental Organisation)
- First smartphone prototype development begins
- Integration of Buckminster Fuller's World Peace Game concepts

## **2019 - Core Development**
- Avatar Karma System development begins (digital twin of users)
- **OASIS API goes open source** (August 2019) - major milestone
- Deep Holochain integration begins
- Core API development accelerates

## **2020 - WEB4 Implementation**
- Anorak's Journey video series begins documenting OASIS creation
- WEB4 OASIS API implementation and testing
- Provider integration expands

## **2021 - STAR ODK Birth**
- **STAR ODK born** on Lions Gate (August 8, 2021)
- Evolution from digital twin of planet to digital twin of Solar System
- WEB5 STAR ODK development begins
- Full ecosystem deployment starts

## **2022-2023 - Production Releases**
- Integration of 50+ Web2 and Web3 providers
- STAR ODK v2.2.0 released (November 2023)
- OASIS Runtime v3.3.1 released (November 2023)
- First production-ready releases

## **2024 - Major Updates**
- **HoloNET v3.0.2 released** - complete rewrite with .NET v8.0
- Over 100,000 lines of code written
- Advanced STAR features and NFT collections
- Style and StarNet development

## **2025 - STAR MVP & Demos**
- **STAR DEMO series** launched (December 2024)
- **STAR MVP release** as Christmas present to community
- Guardians of Infinite Reality NFT Campaign
- Year of growth with big launches ahead

## Key Achievements

✅ **50+ providers** integrated across Web2 and Web3  
✅ **Universal API** connecting everything to everything  
✅ **Auto-failover technology** (OASIS HyperDrive) ensuring 100% uptime  
✅ **Revolutionary NFT system** (Web3, Web4, Web5)  
✅ **Global community** of developers and users  
✅ **Production-ready** Web4/Web5 infrastructure  

From 2011 to 2025, OASIS has transformed from a vision into a working system that revolutionizes how we interact with the internet, eliminating silos and walled gardens through universal connectivity.
