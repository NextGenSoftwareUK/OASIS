# ODOOM Launch Research — Staggered Chapter Launch

**Status:** Early thoughts, scattered; to be cohered and refined later.  
**Purpose:** Capture launch vision, narrative hooks, and script/lore riffs for the interoperable DOOM↔Quake mission series.

## 1. Overall Objective

- **Primary:** Get **gamers**—especially **DOOM and Quake fans**—to experience an **interoperable mission series** that **connects DOOM to Quake for the first time**.
- **Delivery:** A **storyline** released in **chapters** (sequential), where:
  - Players collect **keycards and items** in one game that are **required in the other** (DOOM ↔ Quake).
  - The experience **breaks the 4th wall**: blends into **Telegram**, **X (Twitter)**, and **Discord**—where players retrieve **passwords**, **NFTs**, and other unlocks to progress in-game.
- **Outcome:** Fans feel they are playing a *real* continuation of classic DOOM/Quake, with a single identity and inventory across both games and our channels.

---

## 2. Launch Vision 

### Teaser / Opening

- **Feel:** The teaser should feel **EXACTLY like the original DOOM game**—same tone, same kind of “story so far” and in-game text.
- **Narrative hook (draft):** A **new portal has opened** between the world of DOOM and the world of Quake. Command needs **you** (the space marine) to get in there and **close it** before demons (and worse) spill into our dimension—or before something from the Quake dimensions spills into Hell.
- **Continuity:** Story continues from **existing DOOM lore** (UAC, Phobos, Deimos, gateways, Hell). We can later tie in Quake lore (slipgates, Ranger, Shub-Niggurath’s dimensions) as the portal is explored.

### Chapters

- **Sequential release:** Chapters released **one after another**—not everything at once.
- **Increasing complexity:** Early chapters teach “one identity, one inventory, two games”; later chapters add runes, keycards, and 4th-wall puzzles (passwords, NFTs, community clues).
- **Cross-game items:** Keycards/items collected in **DOOM** are needed in **Quake** (e.g. to open a specific door or trigger a slipgate). And vice versa: items from **Quake** unlock areas or objects in **DOOM**. This forces players to play both and reinforces “one world, two engines.”

### 4th Wall / Community Channels

- **Telegram, X, Discord** are not just “community”—they are **part of the mission**.
- **Passwords:** Some doors or terminals in-game require a **password** or **code** that is only revealed in a specific channel (e.g. Telegram announcement, Discord pin, X thread).
- **NFTs:** Certain items or keys can be **NFTs** (e.g. SPL on Solana via OASIS)—held in wallet, shown in Discord/Telegram, then “used” in-game when the game checks inventory/NFT ownership.
- **Clues and ARG-style:** Riddles, coordinates, or “classified” intel dropped on X or Discord that translate into in-game codes, next chapter unlock, or secret levels.
- **Vibe:** “Command is broadcasting through whatever channels still work—Telegram, Discord, X. Check your comms for the next objective.”

## 3. Lore We Can Use (Summary)

*(Full detail in [DOOM_QUAKE_LORE_REFERENCE.md](./DOOM_QUAKE_LORE_REFERENCE.md).)*

### DOOM

- **UAC**, Mars, **Phobos**, **Deimos**; **gateways** between moons and Hell; “Something fraggin’ evil is coming out of the Gateways!”
- Marine: punished for refusing to fire on civilians; only survivor; **no name** (“it’s supposed to be YOU”).
- **Deimos** is pulled into **Hell**; “THE ONLY WAY OUT IS THROUGH”; eventually **Spiderdemon** mastermind; then “THE GREEN FIELDS OF EARTH! HOME AT LAST” (and the ironic “IT’S GOOD THAT NO HELL-SPAWN COULD HAVE COME THROUGH THAT DOOR WITH YOU…”).
- **Daisy** (pet rabbit) in later canon; “SOMEONE WAS GONNA PAY FOR WHAT HAPPENED TO DAISY.”
- **DOOM II:** Hell on Earth; Icon of Sin / Baphomet; spaceport, flame barrier, evacuation ships.

### Quake

- **Slipgates** = human tech for instant transport; **“Quake”** = enemy from **another dimension** using slipgates to attack Earth; **Operation Counterstrike**; player is **sole survivor**, uses Quake’s own slipgate to pursue.
- **Ranger** (player); four **runes** (e.g. Rune of Black Magic) to reach **Shub-Niggurath’s Pit**.
- **Dimensions:** tech bases → medieval/gothic → **Elder World** (Lovecraft); **Chthon**, **Vore**, **Shub-Niggurath** (Hell-Mother, mother of all monsters).

### Bridge for Our Story

- **DOOM** has **gateways** (UAC/military); **Quake** has **slipgates** (military/scientists). Both are “portal tech that went wrong.”
- A **new** gateway/slipgate that links **Hell** (or UAC space) to **Quake’s dimensions** fits both canons: UAC or remnants discover a rift; or Hell’s incursions have torn into the same dimensional space Quake’s forces use. “Close the portal before demons spill into our dimension” (and/or before Shub-Niggurath’s influence spills into ours).

### DOOM — Manual / “Story So Far” (opening)

- *“We require immediate military support. Something fraggin' evil is coming out of the Gateways! Computer systems have gone berserk!”*
- *“You'll never navigate off the planet on your own. Plus, all the heavy weapons have been taken by the assault team leaving you with only a pistol.”*
- *“Whatever killed your buddies deserves a couple of pellets in the forehead.”*
- *“They know you're here. There's no turning back now.”*

### Quake — Manual / Story

- **Setup:** 4 a.m. call; secret installation; “It's about the Slipgate device. Once we perfect these, we'll be able to use them to transport people and cargo from one place to another instantly.”
- **Enemy:** “An enemy codenamed Quake is using his own slipgates to insert death squads into Earth's bases.” Scientists believe Quake is from **another dimension** and is preparing to unleash a massive army.
- **Operation Counterstrike:** Player is “our best man”; “If the eggheads are right, all our lives are expendable.”
- **Prelude to Destruction:** Base overrun; “Operation Counterstrike is over, except for you.” “The heart of the installation holds a slipgate still set to Quake's dimension.” “You load your shotgun and move forward.”

## 5. Chapter Structure (Preliminary)

*(To be cohered with storyline brief.)*

| Idea | Notes |
|------|--------|
| **Ch 0 / Teaser** | Feels like original DOOM. “New portal DOOM↔Quake; marine must close it.” Short playable or video + text. |
| **Ch 1** | One game only (e.g. DOOM). Introduce OASIS identity, beam-in, one keycard that “doesn’t open anything here yet.” End screen: “THIS KEYCARD IS FOR THE OTHER SIDE.” |
| **Ch 2** | Other game (Quake). Same identity. Door or lock that requires the keycard from Ch 1. First “cross” moment. |
| **Ch 3+** | Alternate; items in DOOM needed in Quake and vice versa. Introduce **password** lock: “CHECK COMMS FOR CODE.” |
| **Later** | NFTs as keys or proof; Discord/Telegram/X drops codes, riddles, or NFT-gated content. |
| **Final** | Climax that uses both games and community intel (e.g. final code assembled from all channels). |
