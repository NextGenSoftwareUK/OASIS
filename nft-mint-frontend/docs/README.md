## üöÄ Quick Start: Your First Outreach

**New to fundraising/outreach? Start here:**

1. **Choose your target audience**
   - Investor? Start with `Outreach_Email_Template.md` Template 1
   - Partner? Start with Template 2
   - Enterprise? Start with Template 3

2. **Customize the email**
   - Research recipient (LinkedIn, Twitter, recent news)
   - Personalize first paragraph with specific reference
   - Adjust use cases to their context
   - Include clear call-to-action

3. **Attach the right document**
   - First contact: `Executive_Summary.md` (or none, just link to demo)
   - After positive response: `OASIS_NFT_Minting_Value_Proposition.md`
   - Technical discussion: `multi-chain-plan.md`

4. **Follow up (but don't spam)**
   - No response after 5 days: Send one follow-up
   - No response after 7 more days: One final follow-up
   - No response after that: Move on (they're not interested)

5. **Track your outreach**
   - Use a simple spreadsheet (Name, Company, Date Sent, Status, Notes)
   - Log responses and schedule follow-ups
   - Note what messaging resonates

## üí° Tips for Success

### **For Investor Pitches:**
- Lead with traction, not vision (investors want proof)
- Be specific about use of funds and milestones
- Show you understand your competition
- Demonstrate domain expertise (why you can execute)
- Have answers ready for: TAM, unit economics, customer acquisition strategy

### **For Partnership Discussions:**
- Focus on mutual benefits (what's in it for them?)
- Propose specific collaboration models
- Show you've done research on their product/ecosystem
- Be flexible and open to creative arrangements

### **For Enterprise Sales:**
- Lead with ROI and cost savings (CFOs care about this)
- Provide social proof (other customers, case studies)
- Offer pilot programs to reduce friction
- Make the buying process as simple as possible

### **For Developer Outreach:**
- Show code examples and documentation quality
- Respect their time (make it easy to integrate)
- Engage in technical communities (Discord, GitHub, Twitter)
- Provide generous free tiers and clear pricing
