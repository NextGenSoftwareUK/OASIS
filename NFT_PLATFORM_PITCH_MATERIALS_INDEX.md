### Presentation Tips

**For Investors:**
- Lead with traction (live demo, real users)
- Focus on TAM and competitive moats
- Show clear path to revenue
- Be specific about use of funds

**For White-Label Clients:**
- Lead with ROI (cost savings)
- Show similar use cases (social proof)
- Emphasize speed to market (2-4 weeks)
- Offer pilot/trial to reduce risk

**For Developers:**
- Lead with API simplicity
- Show code examples
- Emphasize documentation quality
- Offer free tier to test

- Oversell (be honest about limitations)
